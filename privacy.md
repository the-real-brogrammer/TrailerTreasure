# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** January 27, 2026

Trailer Treasure respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected and used when you use the app.

---

## Information We Collect

When you sign in using **Sign in with Apple**, we collect the following information to create and manage your account:

- Name  
- Email address  
- User identifier  

This information is used solely for account functionality within the app.

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information only to:

- Create and manage your account  
- Allow you to save favorites, follow actors, and submit trailers  
- Associate saved content with your account  

We do **not** use your information for advertising, analytics, or marketing purposes.

---

## Data Storage

Your account information and app data (such as favorites and submitted trailers) are stored securely using **Apple CloudKit**.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

---

## Tracking

Trailer Treasure does **not** track users across apps or websites owned by other companies.

---

## Third-Party Services

The app uses The Movie Database (TMDB) API to fetch movie information. Search queries are sent to TMDB only to retrieve movie data and are not used by us for tracking or analytics.

---

## Data Deletion

You can delete your account and associated data directly within the app.  
When an account is deleted, all associated data is permanently removed.
